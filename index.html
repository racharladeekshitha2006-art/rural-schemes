How This Portal Helps Rural Citizens

1. Search by Need

Citizens can search for schemes related to education, farming, health, housing, pensions, and self-employment.

2. Understand Eligibility

Each scheme card clearly shows who can apply, what benefits are provided, and the main target group.

3. Visit Official Portal

Users can directly open the official application or information page from the scheme card.

4. Improve Awareness

This project can reduce the information gap between government welfare schemes and rural beneficiaries.

Project Objective

The objective of this project is to bridge the information gap between rural citizens and Andhra Pradesh government welfare schemes by presenting scheme details in a simple, searchable, and category-based portal. It helps users identify which scheme is suitable for them and where they can apply.
